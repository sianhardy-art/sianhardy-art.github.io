### sian hardy (she/her/they/them) is an emerging visual artist currently living and working on ngunnawal and ngambri country. 

sian's practice works across mediums including painting, drawing, ceramics, textiles and photography. their process draws on sustained explorations of materiality as a mediating and collaborative facet of  building and expressing personal and social relationships with country and place. 


sian is a settler on unceded aboriginal lands.
